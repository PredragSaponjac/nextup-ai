Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This policy explains what information NextUp Services LLC (“NextUp,” “we,” “us”) collects through this website and how we use it. We keep this simple, because we collect very little.

1. Information you give us

If you fill out our contact or booking form, we collect what you choose to provide — typically your name, business name, email address, and your message (for example, what you’d point AI at first). If you book a call, the scheduling tool collects the details needed to set up that meeting.

2. Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our host and the services we use may automatically log basic technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit, for security and to keep the site running. We do not use advertising trackers or sell this data.

3. How we use your information

  • To reply to you and schedule or hold a call;
  • To understand what you need before we talk;
  • To operate, secure, and improve the site;
  • To meet legal obligations if required.

We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we don’t send marketing you didn’t ask for.

4. Service providers

We use a few trusted third parties to run the site and handle your requests — for example, a form-handling provider (to deliver form submissions to us), a scheduling provider (to book calls), a hosting provider, and a web-font provider. These providers process data only to perform their service for us and are subject to their own privacy terms.

5. Data retention

We keep the information you send us only as long as needed to respond, to carry out any work you engage us for, and to meet legal or record-keeping needs — then we delete it.

6. Your choices and rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you’ve given us, or ask us to stop contacting you, at any time. Just email hello@nextupservices.com and we’ll take care of it.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure, and we can’t guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

This site is for business users and is not directed to children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect information from them.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted here, and the “last updated” date will reflect the most recent version.
